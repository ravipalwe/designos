# Design Brief — Solvent

## Product
Solvent is a runway tracker for bootstrapped founders: connect your accounts (or log transactions by hand) and always know one number — how many months you have left. No budgeting theater, no charts you'll never read. It exists to replace the 2am spreadsheet.

## Users & Top Tasks
Solo and small-team founders running on their own money.
1. **Check runway at a glance** — success: the number and its trend readable in under 3 seconds, without interaction.
2. **Log / review transactions** — success: adding a transaction takes one input flow; the list makes anomalies obvious.
3. **Adjust assumptions** — success: change expected burn or income and see runway recompute instantly.

## Taste Profile
warm 3 · minimal 4 · **calm 5** · classic 3 · airy 3
Reading: money is stressful; the product must not be. Calm dominates every trade-off — restrained color, unhurried motion, generous-but-purposeful density. Neither nostalgic nor sci-fi.

## References
- Loved: the *feeling* of a well-kept paper ledger (order, permanence); terminal dashboards (numbers first, zero decoration)
- Rejected: crypto-dashboard neon, gamified confetti finance apps — money isn't a slot machine

## Constraints
- Platform: web app, desktop-first, must hold up at 375px
- Dark mode: direction-dependent — decide at Phase 2 (a dark-native direction is on the table)
- Brand givens: none — name "Solvent" only
- Accessibility: WCAG 2.1 AA minimum, APCA-informed
- Data display: amounts must use tabular figures everywhere
